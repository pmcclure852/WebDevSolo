Why WCC?

We have members of all ages and levels of ability and preferred types of climbing, so there is always something going on to get involved with no matter your skills currently are.

We meet to at various local climbing walls once a week and have outdoor plans almost every weekend of the year.

We organise an overseas climbing adventure every year, and have multi-day events further afield here in the UK several times a year.

We also have an annual social where we all get together and bring our families to celebrate around Christmas time.

The Hut

We are very proud of our hut and it's beautiful location in the North Wales. As a member you are free to book beds for you and your party whenever they're available. Head on over to the hut page for all of the details.
