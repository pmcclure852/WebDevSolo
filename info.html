Membership & Info

Membership

Membership costs £50 for life plus £10 per year to cover administration costs.

We welcome people of all ability levels but you must be safe and experienced in belaying. We do not provide tuition but lots of experience can be gained from coming along to our meets. We're a friendly bunch and always happy to share our collective experience.

If are considering becoming a member, please come along to a couple of meets and get to know us and see if it's for you. A recommendation from an existing member is required when you apply.

Drop us an email if you want to apply and we'll send you an application form to complete.

Our Committee

John Jones - President

John has been with the club for 30 years now, he loves multipitch trad climbing in the mountains of North Wales and never misses our summer trip to Scotland each year.

He says he's a little too old for winter climbing now, but in his heyday he was putting up mixed routes all over the ranges in Scotland.

Jane Hoyle - Treasurer

Jane is our trusted treasurer, she makes sure all of our subs are put to good use and we're keeping afloat.

Jane has been climbing for over 20 years, she loves sport climbing in Spain and her dog, Dylan

Colin Rampart - Membership Secretary

Colin has been looking after all things membership for 3 years now.

Most of all he enjoys gritstone climbing in the peak district during the season and adventurous mountaineering in the summer. He's often spotted getting a tan in the Alps!
